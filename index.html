Our Story

Cake bakery was founded in 2010 by master baker Emily Chen, who was inspired by her grandmother's recipes and a passion for traditional baking methods.

What began as a small operation at the local farmers' market has grown into a beloved community bakery, known for exceptional quality and a commitment to using locally sourced, organic ingredients.

Today, our team of skilled bakers continues Emily's tradition of creating delicious, handcrafted baked goods that bring joy to our community. We believe in the power of good food to bring people together and create lasting memories.

Our mission is simple: to bake with integrity, serve with warmth, and create products that nourish both body and soul.
